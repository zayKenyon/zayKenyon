I am fortunate enough to often be stopped by strangers for a conversation. When working, it's generally out of curiosity as to what I'm using to study.

It's the BOOX Go Tablet 10.3, it's an Android e-ink device with pen recognition.

I'm a university student which means I need to do a lot of pre-reading in preparation for my lectures. I use the Calibre software to manage and organise the different types of documents I need: Textbooks, Lecture Slides, Exercise Sheets, and Problem Class Sheets.

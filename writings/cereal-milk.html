title: cereal milk
created_at: 2024-10-25 03:54
last_modified: 2024-10-25 05:37
type: poetry

i ran out of milk and it made me sad.
my mornings arent the same. i havent
been to the store in days.

the fridge is running empty;
i snack on dry cereal.

hours shift, late nights become early mornings.
i wonder if i should pull an all-nighter, for fun,
i reason.

minutes tick-by now. waiting,
five now, ten more still,
waiting to pass so i can progress
to the next stage.

music on, cant bear thinking about what damage im doing
thinking back to who i was last week.
tyres were deflated, seat was too low, and yet,
i cycled to the store. so why,
when it should be easier,
cant i go?
